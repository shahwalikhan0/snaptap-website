# SnapTap Official Color Palette

## Primary Brand Colors
- SnapTap Blue (Primary): #00A8DE (RESERVED: Exclusive to Navbar & main branding logo)
- SnapTap Blue Dark (Actions/Highlights): #007cae (Use for ALL action buttons, text highlighting, page accents, and UI elements)
- SnapTap Blue Deep (Hover): #006080 (Use for hover states of action buttons)

## Secondary / Grayscale
- Dark Grey (Headings): #2e2e2e
- Medium Grey (Body Text): #555555
- Light Grey (Labels/Hints): #888888
- Background Slate: #F8FAFC (Standard light/slate background)

## Implementation Notes
These are mapped to Tailwind 4 variables:
- `text-snaptap-blue` / `bg-snaptap-blue`
- `text-snaptap-blue-dark` / `bg-snaptap-blue-dark`
